About The CA Final Archive

Built for CA Final students in Nepal

This platform was envisioned, created, and is managed by Kishor Babu Subedi. As a dedicated initiative built specifically for Chartered Accountancy (CA) students preparing for their final level examinations, this website serves as a centralized hub designed to streamline preparation, reduce resource fragmentation, and bring structural ease to one of the most rigorous academic journeys a professional can undertake.

Our Vision & Purpose

The path to qualifying as a Chartered Accountant requires immense dedication, precision, and access to the right educational tools. The sole objective of The CA Final Archive is to bridge this gap. By organizing essential academic resources in one organized, easily accessible place, this platform aims to save students time and allow them to focus entirely on their preparation.

Copyright Disclaimer & Intellectual Property Rights

The CA Final Archive operates strictly with the highest respect for academic integrity, authorship, and legal compliance.

  • No Intention of Infringement: This platform has absolutely no intention of making any author’s, professor’s, or institution’s proprietary materials public for commercial gain or unauthorized distribution.
  • Ownership of Rights: We explicitly state and recognize that all rights, copyrights, and ownership of the study materials, notes, and resources hosted or linked on this platform remain entirely with their respective original authors, educators, and publishers.
  • Role as a Resource Manager: The creator of this website does not claim ownership over any third-party educational content. Our role is strictly limited to managing, curating, and indexing these resources in one place to facilitate ease of access for students.

Strictly Non-Commercial & Educational Intent

This website is a purely non-profit, educational initiative. It is created solely for self-study, reference, and academic collaborative purposes to assist CA Final aspirants in navigating their vast syllabus efficiently.
